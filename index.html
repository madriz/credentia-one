Credentia One is an open document standard that replaces the 20-minute job application with a 10-second file transfer. Fill out your professional data once. Use it everywhere.

How it works

1

Fill

Complete the guided form with your professional history, skills, and compliance data. Every field an employer might ask, handled once.

2

Generate

Download your .credentia.json file. It is generated entirely in your browser. Your data never touches our servers.

3

Apply

Use your Credentia file to apply anywhere. One file replaces hundreds of repetitive forms.

The tailored cover letter often feels like writing a sonnet for a calculator.

In a world where the first gatekeeper is an ATS algorithm rather than a human, the romantic notion of hand-crafting each application falls apart. You are not writing for a person. You are filling fields for a database. Credentia One accepts that reality. You provide your complete professional record in a structured, machine-readable format. The employer's system pulls what it needs. The tailoring happens on their side, not yours.

What this is not

Not an ATS.

Employers keep their preferred system. We standardize the input, not the processing.

Not an identity validator.

We verify that your data is complete and properly structured. Background checks and identity verification remain the employer’s responsibility.

Not a resume database.

Your data lives in your browser and on your device. We store a cryptographic hash to certify authenticity. Nothing else.

Open by design

Credentia One is open source under the Apache 2.0 license. The schema, the candidate portal, and the documentation are all public. The standard belongs to everyone.
